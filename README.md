# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Analysis

## 📊 Project Overview

This project analyzes coffee shop sales data to understand business performance, identify sales trends, and generate insights that can help improve revenue and decision-making.
The analysis was performed using **SQL for data analysis** and **Power BI for visualization and dashboard creation**.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

The objective of this project is to analyze coffee shop sales data to identify key trends, customer purchasing patterns, and product performance.

The project focuses on answering important business questions such as:

* Which products generate the most revenue?
* What are the peak sales hours?
* Which days generate the highest sales?
* Which product categories perform the best?

These insights help businesses make **data-driven decisions to improve sales and optimize product offerings**.

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset contains transactional sales records of a coffee shop.

Key fields include:

* Transaction Date
* Store Location
* Product Category
* Product Name
* Quantity Sold
* Unit Price
* Total Sales

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The following KPIs were analyzed in this project:

### Total Sales

* Calculate total sales for each month
* Analyze month-on-month (MoM) growth
* Compare sales between different time periods

### Total Orders

* Calculate total number of orders
* Identify monthly order trends

### Total Quantity Sold

* Calculate total quantity of products sold
* Analyze monthly sales volume changes

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

The Power BI dashboard includes the following visualizations:

### Calendar Heat Map

* Shows daily sales performance
* Darker colors represent higher sales

### Sales by Weekdays vs Weekends

* Compares sales performance across different days

### Sales by Store Location

* Identifies the best performing store locations

### Sales by Product Category

* Shows which product categories generate the highest revenue

### Top 10 Products by Sales

* Identifies the top performing products

### Sales by Day and Hour

* Highlights peak business hours

## 📊 Project Insights

Some insights from the analysis include:

* Certain product categories contribute significantly to overall revenue
* Sales are higher during specific hours of the day
* Weekends show different sales patterns compared to weekdays
* A small number of products generate a large portion of the total revenue
